USER RESEARCH

to understand the problems of the goal and context of use of potential users I used some blockbusting activity and applied different technicals to better analyze the problem from different perspectives.



Gardeners’ pain points

I began by interviewing three people with 6 months, 18 months, and 36 months of horticultural experience.


What is the major problem to plant their own food?

How they maintain the garden and use what method?


Pain points the gardeners mentioned. The primary takeaways from the gardeners were as follows:


Gardeners want to successfully get gardening information such as watering and seeding based on the local climate

Gardeners feel frustrated when advice is not helping them to solve the problems of the location or conditions of their garden.

Gardeners struggle to remember the complete tasks date.

New gardeners often made mistakes to hurt their plants such as overwatering or over prune based on the online instructions

There are overwhelming variables to grow plants for new gardeners
