GARDENERS HUB

Gardener's hub designs for sustainable gardening that brings friendship to the local community




Contribution: Design Research, UX/UI Design, Visual Design, Interaction Design

Skills and Tools: Interviewing, Storyboarding and Speed dating, Experience Prototyping, Adobe XD, Visual Studio Code, html, css, JavaScript

Methods: Contextual Inquiry, Think-Aloud, Storyboarding, Experience Prototyping

PROBLEMS

Information from the internet for growing plants is very generic. gardeners need to solve their problems base on local microclimate including humidity and temperature for growing conditions. gardeners can easily kill their plants base on internet watering instruction without considering the local climate.

gardeners grow plants during early spring from seed. they normally grow more plants from seeds than they really need with less variety.

SOLUTION

A mobile app and website that provide gardeners to share gardening experience and plants with their neighbors including the following features:


share experience, tools, plants, vegetables
