APPOINTA

An interview scheduling tool

Challenge

arrangement of the interview schedule is complicated
  • Need someone to assign a team member to the interview
  • May need to switch team members when not available
  • It takes time to find the right person within schedule to attend the interview
  • such as levels, teams, etc

Solution

Invite team members to different teams
Setup team member skillset and teams
Sync team member schedule from their calendar
The team members will be able to exchange or pass to the other members
Candidates will be able to reschedule or cancel the interview from their calendar
