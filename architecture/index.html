Most of the projects are from my three-year Master of Architecture program. The main focuses were Design Theories, Methods, and Practices. Projects emphasize the major format, spatial, material, tectonic, social, technological, and environmental determinants of building form. the penultimate studio incorporate integrative thinking that shapes complex architectural design and technical solutions based on design research and studio requirements.
