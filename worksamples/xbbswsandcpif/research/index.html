Concept & Use cases

Service

Book independently
  • Must have an available team member
  • Must set date, time, duration
  • Currently not allowed book more than one service at a time
  • The use case can be "book a haircut service", "book a photography full-shoot"

Add-on

Book with service together
  • Not allowed book independently
  • Duration and price can be zero
  • Allow parallel booking
  • The use case can be "book haircut(service) with nail trim(add-on) and bath(add-on)

Promotion

During booking a single service purchase a package
  • Combination of services and add-ons
  • Set discount and quantity of each service
  • Use case can be "Buy 5 get 1 free or buy 6 get 3 free", "Buy 3 get $20.00 off", "Buy 3 get 20% off or buy 1 get 10% off"

Interview business owner

Chat with business owners

We conducted interviews with pet business owners, and chat about how they handle the business booking and what are the pain points of the current booking journey

“Ideally it would be better to have an app to share our grooming process. Need more social aspects, and interact with clients.”

“I prefer our groomer not able to access clients' information”

“I would love to have clients book appointments on their own, every appointment needs approval ”

“ Social media is great, but text messages with photos are also good. people don't really read their email or pickup the phone calls”

― Tanya Ellis, Dog grooming business owner, writer

“Visual view of boarding kennels, ability to drag/drop for availability based on DAY or entire reservation”

“Grooming notes for each appointment visible when booking and during check-in”

“Blast notification for all checked-in clients and for all clients arriving/departing on an individual day for emergencies, schedule changes, cancellations, weather closings, etc.”

― Lynn Paolillo, Instructor/Groomer at National Cat Groomers Institute

“I won't chat with our customers during grooming. I wanna call back to our customers when I have time”

“I have many no show appointments”

“Sometimes, overlapped booking happens”

“Booking is complicated, they do not know how to choose the right package to book. I won't let customers book on their own”

― katie Hagen, Groomer

“Price and duration always adjust at check-in”

“Our limit is 15 minutes late, if they don't come within the 15 minutes, we can refer them out. Call us if they are going to be late, if we are not busy, we still willing to take the appointment. We have buffer time in between appointments ”

― Victoria Toscano, Groomer
